The Manor: An iconic mansion in the heart of Los Angeles With this Los Angeles pad, there might not be any reason to leave, with everything you need in one sprawling property

The iconic Manor, which was built in 1991 for TV mogul Aaron Spelling in the upscale Holmby Hills neighborhood, has hit the market once again and is listed via JamesEdition for $165 million. The Manor ranks as one of the largest single-family homes in Los Angeles. In total, there are 123 rooms in the 56,000-foot mega-mansion, including 14 bedrooms, 27 bathrooms, a formal dining room with a table for 22 guests, a wine cellar, a tasting room, a library, and a movie theatre.
